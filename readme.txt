== Description ==

Displays most recent posts of your commentators URLs on each of your posts. This can help you know what they wrote just now. 

It will help you to stay in touch and connected along with your faithful commentator by showing only their recent story from the RSS Feed above ypur comment template


== Installation ==


1. Upload the zipped file to yoursite/wp-content/plugins
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
